Employee Attrition & Performance Using Machine Learning!

About

Employees are valuable assets of any organization. Organizations must deal with the problems when trained, skilled and experienced employees leave the organization for better opportunities.

This project will utilize historical employee data, including demographics, job roles, performance metrics, satisfaction surveys, and exit interviews, to create a comprehensive dataset for analysis. Machine learning algorithms, such as logistic regression, decision trees, random forests, and neural networks, will be employed to build predictive models capable of identifying potential churners.
